# Product Requirements Document

## Product Name
Medidata Regulatory Intelligence Platform

## Problem Statement
Medidata teams and customers spend significant effort searching across disconnected systems: product documentation, validation artifacts, Jira, GitHub, release notes, SOPs, customer RFPs, and support history. In regulated clinical trial environments, decisions must be traceable and defensible.

## Product Vision
Create an AI-powered regulatory intelligence layer that can answer complex sales, release validation, and compliance questions by orchestrating multiple specialist agents and producing evidence-backed outputs.

## Primary Users
- Sales Engineers
- Solution Architects
- Customer Success
- Validation/CSV Teams
- Product Managers
- Engineering Leads
- QA/Release Managers
- Pharmaceutical sponsor users

## In-Scope Use Cases

### UC1: Requirement & Solution Validation
Given client RFP requirements, the platform maps requirements to Medidata capabilities, gaps, configuration options, customization needs, roadmap items, and compliance considerations.

### UC2: Release Validation & Compliance Impact
Given a product release, client configuration, or application change, the platform determines impacted features, validation artifacts, test evidence, regulatory risk, and recommended validation activities.

## Out of Scope for MVP
- Autonomous production changes
- Automatic FDA submissions
- Final compliance sign-off without human approval
- Direct modification of Jira/GitHub without explicit approval

## Success Metrics
- 50% reduction in pre-sales requirement analysis time
- 40% reduction in validation package preparation time
- 30% faster customer issue triage for release/compliance questions
- 95% of generated conclusions include traceable evidence
- 100% of high-risk compliance conclusions routed to human approval
